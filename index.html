About Us

Goshen Farms is a leading agricultural manufacturing enterprise strategically located in Udenu, Enugu State. Situated opposite Obollo International Hotel along the Obollo-Nsukka Express Way, we combine traditional farming wisdom with modern manufacturing techniques to produce exceptional agricultural products.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a trusted name in Nigeria's agricultural sector. We take pride in supporting local communities while maintaining international standards in all our operations.
