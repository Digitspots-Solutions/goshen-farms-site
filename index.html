About Us

Nna Prince Gas Plant Solutions, operating as Nna Prince Processing Engineering Limited, is a leading petroleum processing engineering firm based in Udenu, Enugu State. Led by an experienced Petroleum Logistics Manager, we specialize in delivering comprehensive gas plant solutions tailored to the unique demands of Nigeria's oil and gas sector.

Our expertise spans the entire lifecycle of petroleum processing facilities - from initial conceptual design and engineering through to installation, commissioning, and ongoing maintenance support. We combine international best practices with deep local knowledge to ensure every project meets the highest safety and operational standards.

With a commitment to technical excellence and client satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for businesses seeking professional petroleum engineering solutions in the Southeast and across Nigeria.
